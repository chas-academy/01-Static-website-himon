About Us

Our Coffee

At Himon's Café we are passionate about a wonderful coffee experience. All of our coffee is specialty coffee. This means that the coffee always reaches over 80 points on a quality scale from 0-100 points. Only 5% of all coffee in the world reaches this quality level. In other words, we can literally say that our coffee is world-class. Roasting, beans, quality and aromas are important factors in order to proudly serve our guests a really good cup of coffee. All this, however, ends up in the dark if we can not proudly say that the coffee is grown under healthy conditions. In addition, the fact that the coffee will come to its correct taste is the freshness. It's just freshly roasted coffee that has the most delicious flavors and flavors. At our own roasting in Himons basement, our coffee is slowly and carefully praised for the unique character of each bean to reach its full potential. To be sure how fresh our coffee is, notice all our bags with the date of roasting and the time. In addition, all of our coffee is Direct Trade, Single Estate or Single Origin-stamped. They are the three names we use on all our beans to give our guests deeper knowledge and insight about our coffee and its origins.
